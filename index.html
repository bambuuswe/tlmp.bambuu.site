Built for the Modern Trucker

Two Features, One Application: TLMP Utils replaces the need for multiple background programs. Whether you just want to log your company miles via the TrucklineMP API or flex your 70-ton Special Transport load on Discord, it's all handled by one highly optimized C# core.

How it works:
1. Hook: The app reads live binary telemetry frames from the game memory.
2. Sync: Cargo, map location, and truck health are processed locally.
3. Broadcast: Data is split securely—tracking info goes to your VTC dashboard, while visual status updates are pushed instantly to your Discord client.

Privacy First: Your tracking history is safely managed, and the Discord RPC integration only displays what you allow it to. Total control over your footprint, right from the app settings.
