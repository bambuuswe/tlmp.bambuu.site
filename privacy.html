Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle and protect the data collected through this telemetry tracker ("the Service").

1. Data Collection

The Service collects three types of data:

  • Telemetry Data: Information from Euro Truck Simulator 2 (ETS2) collected via your local telemetry agent. This includes:
    • Driving Data: Total mileage (odometer), speed, vehicle status (damage), and location markers.
    • Job & Economic Data: Current job status, cargo information, and detailed income/financial records from your in-game activities.
    • Session Statistics: Average speed, driving time, and historical performance trends.
  • Authentication Data: When you log in via TrucklineMP (OAuth), we access your public profile information (username) and information about your connected VTC (Virtual Trucking Company), in accordance with the scopes you have authorized (profile and vtc:read).

2. Usage of Data

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Display your real-time statistics and historical driving data on your dashboard.
  • Calculate and visualize your economic progress, earnings, and goals.
  • Present accurate information regarding your VTC membership and performance.
  • Analyze session data to help you improve your driving efficiency.

3. Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your driving, financial, and session history is stored locally on your server instance.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data, telemetry, or economic data with third parties.
