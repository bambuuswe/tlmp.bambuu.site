Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle and protect the data collected through this telemetry tracker ("the Service").

1. Data Collection

The Service collects three types of data:

  • Telemetry Data: Information from Euro Truck Simulator 2 (ETS2) collected via your local telemetry agent:
    • Driving Data: Total mileage, speed, damage, and location markers.
    • Job & Economic Data: Cargo, job status, and income records.
    • Session Statistics: Average speed, driving time, performance trends.
  • Authentication Data: When you log in via TrucklineMP (OAuth), we access your public profile (profile) and VTC data (vtc:read).

2. Usage of Data

  • Display real-time statistics and driving history
  • Calculate economic progress and earnings
  • Show VTC performance data
  • Analyze driving sessions for improvement

3. Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Data is stored on your server instance
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell or share personal data
