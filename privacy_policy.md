# Privacy Policy
**Last updated: July 6, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle and protect the data collected through this telemetry tracker ("the Service").

### 1. Data Collection
The Service collects two types of data:
* **Telemetry Data:** Information from Euro Truck Simulator 2 (ETS2) collected via your local telemetry agent. This includes, but is not limited to, total mileage (odometer), speed, vehicle status (damage), cargo information, and current job status.
* **Authentication Data:** When you log in via TrucklineMP (OAuth), we access your public profile information (username) and information about your connected VTC (Virtual Trucking Company), in accordance with the scopes you have authorized (`profile` and `vtc:read`).

### 2. Usage of Data
We use the collected data exclusively to:
* Display your real-time statistics and historical driving data on your dashboard.
* Calculate and visualize your progress toward your set goals.
* Present accurate information regarding your VTC membership.

### 3. Storage and Security
* **Local Storage:** Your driving mileage history is stored locally on your server instance.
* **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or telemetry data with third parties.
